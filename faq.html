Booking & Reservation

Booking with Apex Performance is simple! You can book online through our website, call our team at +971 54 492 3609, or visit our office in Dubai. You'll need to provide your driver's license, passport, and credit card. We offer flexible booking options for daily, weekly, and monthly rentals.

Our minimum rental period is 1 day. We also offer special rates for weekly and monthly rentals. Whether you need a car for a few hours or several months, we have flexible options to suit your needs.

You'll need:

• Valid passport or national ID

• Valid driver's license (international license recommended)

• Credit card for security deposit

• UAE visa (if applicable)

Visitors from other countries may need additional documentation. Please contact us for specific requirements.

Driving & Rules

The minimum age is 21 years old for most vehicles. For luxury and sports cars, the minimum age is 25 years old. A valid driver's license must be held for at least 1 year. Young driver surcharge may apply for drivers aged 21-25.

Yes! You can drive throughout the UAE including Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, and other emirates. For international travel outside the UAE, special permissions and insurance are required. Please notify us in advance and we'll arrange Schengen-approved insurance if you're planning to travel to neighboring countries.

You are responsible for any traffic violations incurred during the rental period. Fines and penalties are your responsibility. We'll receive notices from the authorities and forward them to you. It's crucial to follow all UAE traffic laws and speed limits for your safety and to avoid fines.

Yes, to protect our fleet:

Damage & Insurance

If the car sustains damage:

• Report immediately: Call us right away at +971 54 492 3609

• Document the damage: Take photos and notes

• Insurance coverage: Comprehensive insurance covers accidental damage

• Deductible: AED 2,000 per claim may apply

Contact our customer service team for immediate assistance.

Our comprehensive insurance covers:

• Collision damages

• Theft protection

• Third-party liability

• Vandalism

• Natural disasters

We recommend reviewing the full insurance terms with our team.

In case of an accident:

1. Keep everyone safe first

2. Call police (999) for official report

3. Contact us immediately at +971 54 492 3609

4. Do not leave the scene

5. Gather information from other drivers/witnesses

6. Take photos of the scene and vehicle damage

Our insurance will cover eligible claims as per policy terms.
