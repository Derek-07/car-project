Booking Terms & Conditions

Requirements

Documents for Residents:

  • Passport
  • Valid Driver's License
  • International Driver's Permit (required from certain countries)

Documents for UAE Residents:

  • Emirates ID
  • UAE Driver's License

Age Requirement

  • Minimum age for SUVs and Sedans: 18 years old
  • Minimum age for Luxury vehicles: 21 years old

Security Deposit

  • A security deposit of AED 8,000 is required
  • The security deposit will be refunded after 28 days from the return date to your bank account
  • In case of damage, the security deposit will be held for the rental period plus additional charges

Payment Method

  • Cash: AED, USD, GBP, AED and Euro are accepted
  • Credit/Debit Card
  • Payment Link
  • Crypto

Rental Period

  • The minimum rental period is 1 day of 24 hours
  • Rental rates vary based on duration
  • A flat at a minimum of 2500 AED
  • Late returns may be subject to additional charges

Delivery and Collection

  • Free delivery and collection are available within Dubai
  • Delivery and collection in other Emirates will be chargeable

Vehicle Condition and Usage

  • The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as delivered
  • Renter is responsible for any damage, scratches, or major dents
  • Fuel tank must be refilled before return
  • Any violations of traffic rules may result in additional fines

Insurance Coverage

  • Comprehensive insurance is included in the rental price
  • Renter is liable for collision damage exceeding AED 3,000
  • Third-party liability is covered up to applicable limits

Cancellation and Refund Policy

  • Cancellations made 7 days before booking will receive a full refund
  • Cancellations made 3-7 days before will receive 50% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 3 days before will receive no refund
  • No cancellations are permitted after the rental starts

Penalties and Violations

  • Traffic fine violations will be charged to the renter
  • Excessive speeding may result in vehicle confiscation
  • Smoking inside the vehicle will incur a fine of AED 500
  • Drink and drive violations are strictly prohibited and will void all insurance coverage

Restrictions

  • Vehicles must not be driven outside UAE borders without prior written approval
  • Renting the vehicle to a third party is prohibited
  • Off-road driving is not permitted (for applicable vehicle types)

Liability and Damages

  • Renter is responsible for all damages incurred during the rental period
  • Apex Performance Car Rental is not liable for personal belongings left in the vehicle
  • In case of accident, renter must report to police and Apex within 24 hours

Modification and Amendments

Apex Performance Car Rental reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time without prior notice. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of any modifications.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from the rental agreement will be resolved according to the laws of the United Arab Emirates. Both parties agree to submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Dubai.

Acknowledgment
