Welcome to Apex Performance Car Rental

These Terms and Conditions ("Agreement") establish the legal relationship between you and Apex Performance Car Rental ("us", "we", "our") regarding the rental of our luxury vehicles. By completing the booking process and accepting this agreement, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all terms contained herein.

1. Eligible Renters

  • Minimum Age: You must be at least 21 years old at the time of rental. For premium vehicles (supercars, exotic cars), minimum age requirement is 25 years.
  • Valid Driver's License: A valid driver's license (national or international) must be presented. Your license must have been valid for at least 2 years.
  • Valid Passport: An original, valid passport or emirates ID must be provided for identification verification.
  • Visa Status: For international visitors, a valid visa stamp in your passport is required.

2. Security Deposit

  • Deposit Amount: A refundable security deposit is required for all rentals. Standard luxury vehicles require AED 5,000; supercars/exotic vehicles require AED 10,000 - 25,000.
  • Payment Method: Security deposit must be paid via credit/debit card. Cash deposits are accepted with manager approval.
  • Refund Policy: The deposit will be refunded within 7 business days after return of the vehicle in acceptable condition, provided there are no damages, traffic violations, or outstanding charges.
  • Damage Deduction: We reserve the right to deduct repair costs from the security deposit for any damage beyond normal wear and tear.

3. Payment Terms

  • Rental Rates: Rates are per calendar day (24 hours). Partial days are charged at full daily rate. Monthly and seasonal discounts may apply.
  • Payment Schedule: 50% advance payment is required at booking confirmation. Remaining balance is due at vehicle pickup.
  • Late Return Charges: Late return charges will be applied at AED 500 per hour (or full day rate if exceeding 2 hours).
  • Accepted Payment Methods: Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Bank Transfers, Mobile Wallets.

4. Vehicle Condition & Documentation

  • Pre-Rental Inspection: Vehicle will be inspected before handover. Renter must verify condition and sign the inspection report.
  • Fuel Policy: Vehicle is provided with full fuel tank. Return vehicle with full fuel tank to avoid AED 200 refueling charge + fuel cost at market rate.
  • Vehicle Documentation: Original registration, insurance, and safety certificates provided. Upon return, these documents must be returned intact.
  • Mileage Limit: Unlimited mileage included. Excessive mileage beyond 200 km per day may incur additional charges.

5. Rental Period & Pickup/Return

  • Minimum Rental Period: Minimum 24 hours for daily rentals. Weekly and monthly packages available at discounted rates.
  • Pickup Times: Standard pickup is between 08:00 AM - 08:00 PM. Outside these hours incurs AED 150 additional charge.
  • Return Deadline: Vehicle must be returned by 11:00 PM unless prior arrangement is made.
  • Early Return: No refund for early return. Pay for reserved period.

6. Insurance & Liability

  • Basic Insurance: Comprehensive insurance is included in all rental rates covering third-party liability and physical damage. Excess/Deductible: AED 3,000.
  • Premium Insurance: Optional Premium Insurance available (Excess waiver) for AED 500/day reduces deductible to AED 500.
  • Renter Liability: Renter is liable for all damages resulting from negligence, misuse, or traffic violations. Intentional damage not covered by insurance must be paid in full.
  • Accident Procedure: Contact us immediately. Do not admit liability. Report to police. Provide accident report and photos.

7. Damage & Additional Charges

  • Minor Damage: Scratches, dents, broken lights: AED 1,500 - 5,000 depending on severity.
  • Major Damage: Windshield, body work, frame damage: Full repair cost charged to renter.
  • Tire Damage: Punctured/damaged tire: AED 800 - 2,000 per tire depending on vehicle and liability.
  • Cleaning Fee: If vehicle returns in unacceptable condition requiring professional detail: AED 500.
  • Lost or Damaged Items: Renter responsible for all lost/damaged items left in vehicle.

8. Traffic Violations & Fines

  • Renter Responsibility: All traffic violations, speeding tickets, parking fines, and toll charges incurred during rental period are the renter's responsibility.
  • Fine Processing: Fines will be forwarded to renter after receiving from authorities. Administrative processing fee: AED 100 per violation.
  • Serious Violations: Traffic violations (rash driving, DUI, accidents) may result in vehicle forfeiture and vehicle recovery fees.

9. Vehicle Use Restrictions

  • Permitted Use: Vehicle for personal, recreational, and business use only. Off-road driving strictly prohibited.
  • Prohibited Uses: Commercial transportation, taxi service, racing, transportation of hazardous materials, towing operations, or any illegal activity.
  • Passenger Limit: Maximum occupants as specified in vehicle documentation. Exceeding capacity voids insurance.
  • Smoking & Pets: Smoking inside vehicle strictly prohibited (fine: AED 500). Pets only with prior written approval.
  • Geographic Restrictions: Vehicle must remain in UAE unless prior written approval is obtained.

10. Cancellation & Modifications

  • Free Cancellation: Cancel within 14 days before rental: 100% refund minus processing fee (AED 250).
  • Partial Refund: Cancel 7-14 days before rental: 50% refund of rental charges.
  • No Refund: Cancel within 7 days or after rental begins: No refund. Security deposit remains intact.
  • Date Modifications: Rental date changes are subject to vehicle availability and applicable rate adjustments.

11. Driver Conduct & Safety Compliance

  • Seatbelts & Safety: All occupants must wear seatbelts. Child safety seats required for children under 12 years.
  • Intoxication: Driving under the influence is strictly prohibited and illegal. Rental immediately terminated.
  • Reckless Driving: We reserve right to terminate rental immediately for reckless or dangerous driving.
  • Mobile Phone Use: Hands-free calling only while driving. No text messaging while operating vehicle.

12. Limitation of Liability & Dispute Resolution

  • Liability Disclaimer: Apex Performance is not liable for personal injury, lost or damaged personal property, or indirect damages.
  • Dispute Resolution: All disputes will be resolved through arbitration in Dubai, UAE under UAE law.
  • Governing Law: This agreement governed by laws of United Arab Emirates. Dubai courts have exclusive jurisdiction.

13. Future Updates

Apex Performance reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time. Updated terms will be posted on our website. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of updated terms. We recommend reviewing this document periodically for changes. Last Updated: January 2025.
